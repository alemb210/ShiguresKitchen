About Shigure's Kitchen

Shigure's Kitchen is a Japanese restaurant located inside the Izu Animal Kingdom food court. The restaurant features a variety of dishes inspired by the white tiger Shigure (時雨) and his sister Tsuki (月). The twins were born on September 12, 2024, and are two of Japan's most famous white tigers. To bring awareness to the endangered species and fund preservation efforts for the beautiful ivory species, Shigure's Kitchen was founded. Shigure's kitchen donates a portion of its proceeds to the Izu Animal Kingdom's conservation fund.
